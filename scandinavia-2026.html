Itinerary

13 days · Preliminary Itinerary
01
Sun, 5 Jul 2026
Arrival in Bergen
Keyser Apartments 8
Travel day
▾
Morning
  • 08:20 Fly AMS → BGO (KLM KL1163)
  • 10:00 Arrive Bergen Flesland Airport
  • Take the Bergen Light Rail (Bybanen) from airport to city center (~45 mins)
Afternoon
  • 15:00 Check into Keyser Apartments 8 (Self check-in via lockbox)
  • Explore Bryggen (UNESCO World Heritage wooden houses)
  • Fløibanen Funicular — ride up Mount Fløyen for spectacular city views and let the kids play at the Troll Forest playground at the top.
02
Mon, 6 Jul 2026
Exploring the Fjord Capital
Keyser Apartments 8
City day
▾
Morning
  • Bergen Fish Market (Torget) — grab fresh pastries and browse the local catches.
  • Bergen Aquarium — perfect for the 3 kids to see penguins and local sea life.
Afternoon
  • Wander the cobblestone streets of Nordnes peninsula.
  • Dinner with all 3 families in central Bergen.
03
Tue, 7 Jul 2026
Into the Mountains
ChaletMons, Myrkdalen
Travel day
▾
Morning
  • Check out from Keyser Apartments.
  • Take the Bergen Railway from Bergen to Voss (approx 1h 15m scenic ride).
Afternoon
  • Transfer from Voss to Myrkdalen (bus or pre-arranged taxi).
  • 15:00 Check into ChaletMons. Settle into the massive 4-bedroom house.
  • Grocery run to stock the chalet for the next 4 nights.
04
Wed, 8 Jul 2026
Myrkdalen Alpine Exploring
ChaletMons, Myrkdalen
Nature day
▾
Day
  • Enjoy the fresh mountain air. Take local hiking trails directly from the resort.
  • If the summer chairlift is operating, ride up for expansive valley views.
  • Kids can play in the open nature areas. Big family dinner at the Chalet in the evening.
05
Thu, 9 Jul 2026
Vikings & Nærøyfjord
ChaletMons, Myrkdalen
Kids highlight
▾
Day
  • Day trip to Gudvangen (approx 30-40 min drive/bus).
  • Njardarheimr Viking Valley — an immersive, authentic Viking village. Great interactive experience for the kids (ages 6, 8, 9).
  • Afternoon: Optional spectacular boat cruise through the Nærøyfjord (UNESCO protected narrow fjord) from Gudvangen to Flåm.
Notes
  • 📋 If doing the Fjord Cruise, pre-booking is highly recommended in July!
06
Fri, 10 Jul 2026
The Flåm Railway
ChaletMons, Myrkdalen
Scenic day
▾
Day
  • Head down to Flåm.
  • Ride the Flåmsbana (Flåm Railway) up to Myrdal and back. Known as one of the most beautiful train journeys in the world.
  • Last night in the Myrkdalen Chalet — pack up.
07
Sat, 11 Jul 2026
Across the Roof of Norway
Citybox Oslo
Travel day
▾
Morning
  • 12:00 Check out of ChaletMons.
  • Transfer back to Voss Train Station.
Afternoon
  • Board the Bergen Railway towards Oslo. This is a 5.5 hour journey over the Hardangervidda plateau — spectacular alpine scenery.
  • Arrive Oslo Central Station in the evening.
Evening
  • Check into Citybox Oslo (very central, easy walk from station).
  • Grab a late dinner near Karl Johans gate.
08
Sun, 12 Jul 2026
Polar Ships & Sculptures
Citybox Oslo
City day
▾
Morning
  • Take the ferry to the Bygdøy peninsula.
  • Visit the Fram Museum — board the strongest wooden polar ship ever built. Incredible for kids and adults alike.
Afternoon
  • Vigeland Sculpture Park — stroll through the world's largest sculpture park by a single artist. Great open space for the kids.
  • Explore the modern Aker Brygge area for dinner.
09
Mon, 13 Jul 2026
Train to Sweden
Scandic No 53, Stockholm
Travel day
▾
Morning
  • Check out of Citybox Oslo before 12:00.
  • Walk to Oslo S, board the SJ High-Speed train to Stockholm (approx 5.5 hours).
Afternoon
  • Enjoy the Swedish countryside sliding by.
  • Arrive at Stockholm Central.
  • 16:00+ Check into Scandic No 53 (very close to the station).
Evening
  • First walk around Stockholm's Norrmalm district.
10
Tue, 14 Jul 2026
Vasa & The Old Town
Scandic No 53, Stockholm
Highlight
▾
Morning
  • Vasa Museum — the fully intact 17th-century ship that sank on her maiden voyage. The absolute must-see in Stockholm.
Afternoon
  • Gamla Stan (Old Town) — explore the narrow cobblestone streets, colorful buildings, and the Royal Palace.
  • Enjoy Swedish meatballs or a local Fika (coffee and cinnamon buns).
11
Wed, 15 Jul 2026
Cruising the Baltic
Silja Serenade Cruise
Overnight ship
▾
Morning
  • Check out of Scandic No 53 by 12:00. Store luggage if needed.
  • Junibacken — kids' attraction dedicated to Astrid Lindgren (Pippi Longstocking) on Djurgården.
Afternoon
  • Head to Värtahamnen ferry terminal.
  • 16:45 Silja Serenade cruise departs Stockholm. Sail out through the beautiful Stockholm archipelago (thousands of islands).
Evening
  • 18:00 Dinner reservation at Tavolàta Ristorante Italiano on board.
  • Enjoy the ship's promenade, kids club, and tax-free shopping.
  • Sleep in B-Standard Inside Cabins.
12
Thu, 16 Jul 2026
Hello Helsinki
TBD Helsinki Hotel
City day
▾
Morning
  • 10:30 Ship arrives at Helsinki Olympia Terminal.
  • Disembark and transfer to TBD Helsinki Hotel to drop bags.
Afternoon
  • Take the short ferry to Suomenlinna Sea Fortress. Explore the tunnels and cannons — highly recommended for kids.
  • Dinner in central Helsinki.
13
Fri, 17 Jul 2026
Departure Day
Fly HEL → AMS
Travel day
▾
Morning
  • Helsinki Cathedral & Senate Square photo ops.
  • Browse the Kauppatori (Market Square) for last-minute souvenirs and local berries.
Afternoon
  • Head to Helsinki-Vantaa Airport (HEL).
  • 18:25 Fly HEL → AMS (KLM KL1254).
  • 19:55 Arrive in Amsterdam.
