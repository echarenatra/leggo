Notes
  • ✓ Loch Ness Boat Cruise: pre-booked and paid. Freedom Cruise & Castle 2h departing Clansman Hotel Harbour 13:00.

  • ⚠️ Jacobite Steam Train 2026: as of April 2026, the operator (West Coast Railways) has not confirmed public bookings. There is an ongoing dispute over safety regulations for the vintage carriages. Posters at Fort William station indicate the morning service runs 1 Apr – 23 Oct 2026 (departing Fort William ~10:15, crossing the viaduct ~10:45). Check westcoastrailways.co.uk weekly for updates. If the steam train is not running, the ScotRail diesel service runs the same route and crosses the same viaduct — you'll still see a train on the arches, just without the Harry Potter steam.

Fri, 24 Apr 2026
Skye — West Coast: Castles, Pools & Black Sand
Hebridean - Rooms & Apartments
Kids highlight
▾
Morning
  • 08:30 Early start — drive from Sleat to Dunvegan (~50 min)
  • 09:30 Dunvegan Castle — skip the castle interior. Head straight to the jetty for the seal boat.
  • 09:45 Seal Colony Boat Trip — depart from the castle jetty, see wild grey seals up close. This is the highlight — the castle interior is not worth the entry fee.
  • 10:45 Drive south to Carbost (~30 min)
Afternoon
  • 11:15 LUNCH: The Old Inn, Carbost (⭐ 4.6) — famous traditional pub lunch right on the water, spacious and excellent for families.
  • 12:45 Drive to Fairy Pools (~15 min) — 40 min walk beside crystal-clear turquoise mountain pools. Perfectly manageable for a 6yo.
  • 14:45 Coffee Break: Cafe Cùil (⭐ 4.8) — incredible, spacious Nordic/Scottish café near Carbost to warm up after the pools.
  • 15:45 Talisker Bay — easy 20 min flat walk to a spectacular black sand beach enclosed by towering sea cliffs
Evening
  • 17:30 Drive from Talisker Bay area
  • 18:00 DINNER: Taigh Ailean Hotel Restaurant (⭐ 4.6) — spacious, highly-rated local food near Minginish to wrap up the West Coast day before resting.
  • 19:30 Optional: Neist Point Lighthouse for golden hour. Drive ~30 min, 40 min walk return. Atlantic views, seabirds. If the family has energy and sky is clear — go.
  • Drive back to Hebridean. Prepare for a very early morning tomorrow.
Notes
  • 📋 Dunvegan Castle seal boat trips: runs from April but early season — call Dunvegan Castle ahead to confirm availability and operating days. Lower demand — no urgent pre-booking, but check closer to the trip. You do not need a castle entry ticket to book the seal boat.
  • ✂️ If time runs short: Talisker Bay is the most skippable — Fairy Pools already delivers the magical water experience. Seal boat + Fairy Pools are the two essentials.
  • ⚠️ Day 8 requires a 06:30 departure. Pack tonight, set alarms, prepare coffee.
