  • ~15:00 Boat returns to Clansman Harbour

Notes
  • ✓ Loch Ness Boat Cruise: pre-booked and paid. Freedom Cruise & Castle 2h departing Clansman Hotel Harbour 13:00. The boat includes both a 1-hour cruise and a 1-hour castle visit — you disembark at Urquhart Castle and re-board.
  • ℹ️ Urquhart Castle entry is included in the boat ticket — no separate ticket needed.
  • ℹ️ Free parking at Clansman Harbour, but it can be limited in peak season. If redirected to overflow parking, allow 15 min walk on uneven terrain to the harbour. Arrive 45 min before departure.
  • ⚠️ Jacobite Steam Train 2026: as of April 2026, the operator (West Coast Railways) has not confirmed public bookings. There is an ongoing dispute over safety regulations for the vintage carriages. Posters at Fort William station indicate the morning service runs 1 Apr – 23 Oct 2026 (departing Fort William ~10:15, crossing the viaduct ~10:45). Check westcoastrailways.co.uk weekly for updates. If the steam train is not running, the ScotRail diesel service runs the same route and crosses the same viaduct — you'll still see a train on the arches, just without the Harry Potter steam.
